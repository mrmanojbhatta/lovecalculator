About Love Calculator

This love calculator is designed to calculate the compatibility between two people based on their names. While it's meant for fun, many people enjoy seeing what percentage they get with their loved ones!

How It Works

The calculator analyzes the letters in both names and calculates a compatibility score based on:

  • Letter values (A=1, B=2, ..., Z=26)
  • Combined name length
  • Matching initials
  • Similar last names
